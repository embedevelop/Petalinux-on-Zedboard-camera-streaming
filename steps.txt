Bhmata tou project

1) Katalabame pws doyleuei h ov7670
2) Allaksame to top module se verilog kai enswmatosame thn leitoyrgia poy pagwnei thn eikona
3) Dhmioyrghsame ena IP me to project ayto kai testarame thn leitoyrgia toy se vga
4) Grapsame diko mas software poy epikoinwnei mesw axi kai gia na stelnontai apo auto oi entoles gia pagwma ths eikonas
5) mathame pws leitoyrgoyn ta petalinux kai pws ftiaxnoyme dika mas apps gia na ta trexoume ekei
6) Melethsame ta specs poy xreiazetai ena Zynq ps gia na einai bootable ta petalinux se ayta
7) Parapanw prosoxh xreiasthke sta components prokeimenoy na leitoyrgoyn (ta mio den einai prosbashma sto PL dioti einai desmeymena sto apo to PS)
8) Dhmiourghsame diko mas hardware kai vrikame thn diadikasia pws na to enswmatonoyme to systhma poy trexei petalinux
9) Mathame pws ginetai h antistoixhsh ths psysical address twn peripheriakwn prokeimenoy na ta kanoyme access apo thn efarmogh mas sta petalinux
10) Melethsame ta socket kai tis leitoygries toys
11) Grapsame se C enan server kai ena client kai mathame pws na metaferoyme dedomane(char array) me epityxia apo ton ena ston allo
12) Prokeimenoy na elegksoyme thn eikona poy kanei capture h FPGA vrhkame enan tropo dhmioyrgias eyxrwmoy arxeioy eikonas-ppm format
13) Steilame me epityxia mia eikona se ppm format apo ton server ston client me ton client na thn anakataskeyasei
14) Grapsame enan server o opoios trexei sthn FPGA, kanei capture mia statikh eikona kai thn stelnei ston client o opoios dhmioyrgei to arxeio eikonas 
15) Prokeimenou na dhmioygrhsoyme to stream grapsame enan client se Python o opoios se kathe nea eikona ananewnei to parathyro ths eikonas xrhsimopoiwntas kapoies grafikes vivliiothikes
		phgame se python dioti h diadikasia ayth se C einai arketa poliploki sto na vrethoyn oi vivliothikes oi opoies na kanoyn ayto akribws poy thelame. Thn dhmioyrgia dld enws updatable parathyroy eikonas
16) Ftiaksame mia epikoinwnia typoy handshake metaksi server kai client prokeimenoy o server na pagwnei thn camera, na grafei kai na stelnei thn caprured image MONO otan o client exei 
		teleiwsei me thn ananewsh toy parathyroy eikonas. H diadikasia ayth ginetai pleon me epanalhptiko tropo.
17) To gegonos oti h camera kanbei caprure 8-bit format enw to parathyro eikonas exei 24-bit, dhmioygoyse bottleneck ston client kata thn metatropi ayth. Gia ton logo ayto 
		allaksame to hardware wste sthn eksodo toy na stelnei pleon t0 24-bit format ths eikonas. 
18) Parathrhsame oti otan stelnoyme 4 bytes thn fora apo ton server dhmioyrgeitai problhma me to alignment twn paketwn me apotelesma otan se 24-bit format stelname ta R G B R 
		o client dexotan R B G R. Ayto to lysame me thn diapistwsh oti to network einai big endian enw o arm einai little. Etsi me thn xrhsh ths entolhs htonl ta paketa poy stelname pleon
		htan symfwna me thn leitoyrgia toy network.
